About Me!

I'm currently a second-year student at the University of Michigan’s School of Information. Before being admitted to the BSI program, I was enrolled in LSA Honors where I took courses in psychology, statistics, data oriented programming, web development, and digital product design. Although I have a wide range of professional interests, I am particularly interested in the intersection of technology and human behavior. In my free time, I like to keep up with current events, watch sports and Netflix, and read science fiction books.


I was born in Jackson, Michigan but moved to Potterville, Michigan, and graduated from Potterville High School in 2019. When I was in high school, my favorite classes were chemistry, philosophy, and geometry, however I found a particular interest in Java programming during my senior year when I took APCS.


This summer, I will be working at Quicken Loans as a UX Design intern, and I am currently searching for a Summer 2022 internship in design, consulting, or product management. I have become increasingly interested in startups and consumer technology. Some of my skills include user research, strategy, empathizing, prototyping, design thinking, and project management. To see some of my specific works please visit the “Home” page.
